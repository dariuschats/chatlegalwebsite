Business solutions

Legal solutions and advice for business owners, small and large.

Having acted for various business owners including:

  • 'mom and pop' shops';
  • small to medium businesses;
  • health professionals;
  • start-ups
  • large independent supermarket operators; and
  • large property management firms,

we wanted to bring the beenfits of an 'in-house' lawyer to businesses in an affordable manner.

As a fellow business owner, we understand that your mind and energy is focused on the continued operation and expansion of your business. As such, there is a lack of time able to be spent in considering or appreciating the legal specifics relevant to changes to your business. As your 'in-house' lawyer, our goal is to assist you with considering the legal implication of these changes so that you can focus on what you do best - running your business.

We provide legal advice in relation to various aspects of a business, including:

  • setting up your business;
  • engaging third parties;
  • engaging employees and contractors;
  • expanding your business;
  • introducing new partners in your business;
  • restructuring your business; and
  • selling your business.

We want to see your business grow and succeed, and to be the trusted lawyers by your business' side to ensure you are aware of the potential legal issues that may be relevant for your business.
