Estate planning packages

You choose the level of service you require for your estate plan.

We offer a variety of estate planning packages so that you can pick the level of service that suits your needs.

Our estate planning packages can be summarised as follows:

Package Who for
Bronze For those individuals and families who require the simplest of estate plans; know what you want; and do not require a second meeting. You are able to arrange for your documents to be printed, signed and witnessed pursuant to signing and witnessing instructions provided.
Silver For those individuals and families who do not know where to start and require an initial estate planning meeting to decide how your estate plans are prepared. You are able to arrange for your documents to be printed, signed and witnessed pursuant to signing and witnessing instructions provided.
Gold For those individuals and families who may either have complicated arrangements and/or would like the traditional full service provision of legal services (with bound documents and assistance with signing).
Platinum For those individuals and families who want the lawyer to drive the process in implementing the estate plan and setting up the necessary arrangements to collate the important estate planning documents for executors and family members to be aware of.

All our estate planning packages ensures that holistic estate planning advice is provided and the same style of documents are generated regardless of the package chosen. The only difference between the packages is the level of legal advice or assistance that you may require. Whether it is assistance with signing your estate planning documents; undertaking searches; binding or summarising the advice and documents put in place, you can choose the level that suits your needs.
