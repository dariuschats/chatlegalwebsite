Our service commitment to you

It is our commitment to provide our legal solutions and advice to you in the most pain free manner as possible. We achieve this by collaborating with you and your advisors to tailor the delivery of our services in a manner that aligns with what you want and expect.

Timely

No one likes to wait around for food or other services, so why should legal solutions and advice be any different. If we cannot respond to your email or get you your documents prepared in a timely and reasonable manner, then we have failed in our service commitment to you.

Convenience

We understand how busy life, kids and business can get. This is why we offer our advice and availability to you at a time convenient to you (whether during or after standard office hours) in a manner convenient to you (whether online, over the phone, in person at our office or at a location of your choosing).

Technology

We leverage technology to enable us to deliver our legal solutions and advice in a timely and convenient manner. This allows us to create efficiencies and gives us the confidence to only offer fixed prices for our legal solutions and advice.

Fixed prices

We don’t like unexpected invoices, so why should you. We only commence work once a fixed price is agreed for a legal solution or advice, and the preferred method of delivery. If instructions or circumstances change, we will not invoice you for more than the fixed price unless a change in the price is agreed beforehand.

Meet our team

Darius Hii - Legal Practitioner Director
LLB, BCom(Finance), Member of the Queensland Law Society, Chartered Tax Adviser (Taxation Institute of Australia), Member of the Society of Trusts and Estate Practitioners

Having worked alongside some of Australia’s best tax and estate planning lawyers, Darius founded Chat Legal with the goal of spreading essential legal knowledge to others. With a young family of his own, Darius understands the importance of structuring his affairs (both personal and business) for the benefit of his loved ones and has built his practice around ensuring his clients appreciate this too. Having seen how costly and stressful issues arising from poor tax and estate planning are on clients, Darius is a firm believer of “prevention over cure”. Darius’ hope is to never be required to resolve issues arising from poor planning and makes it his focus to be that trusted advisor to prevent poor decisions from being made. This means spending that extra bit of time explaining difficult legal concepts to clients so that they are able to avoid making common faults in estate planning and business and investment structuring. When not spending time with his family, Darius can be found up late on weekends (and extra early on some weekdays) listening to the audio commentary of European football (soccer) games.

For health professionals

You are a health professional looking for a lawyer who understands the various arrangements and tax issues faced in your profession. Our founder has assisted practitioners and business owners in the health industry since entering the legal profession, and keeps up to date with changes to the tax rules that is relevant. We understand the difficulty in obtaining convenient legal advice and offer various alternatives in the delivery of our services.

Estate planning

You are looking to ensure that you know what happens to your assets when you pass; and wanting to ensure those assets go to the right people. We believe in providing holisitic estate planning advice and undertake in explaining to you the various succession and tax issues that could be relevant to you, so that you are able to make properly informed decisions regarding your estate plan and the options available.

Online document solutions

You know what you want; and you know it's a stock and standard document. We use technology to offer legally binding documents with legal review; faster,cheaper and entirely done online.
