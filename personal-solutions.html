Personal solutions

Legal solutions and advice for individuals and families.

We want to be your trusted legal advisor during your journey called 'life'. Having acted for a wide range of individuals including:

  • young professionals;
  • 'nuclear' families;
  • blended families;
  • small to medium sized business owners; and
  • high-net wealth individuals,

we understand the various legal issues that can arise for you.

We provide legal advice in relation to a range of life's events, including:

  • preparing your tax return;
  • starting a job;
  • investing in property or shares;
  • taking out a loan;
  • protecting your assets;
  • getting married and having a family;
  • entering into a de facto relationship;
  • creating structures such as trusts and companies;
  • understanding structures such as trusts and companies and ensure they are up to date;
  • starting and running a business;
  • planning for expansion;
  • planning for retirement; and
  • planning for death.

We want to be the second number you call (after your loved ones) if any of the above may apply to you, so that you can consider and appreciate the potential legal issues that may be relevant for you.
